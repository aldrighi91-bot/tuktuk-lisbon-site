Full City Tour

The complete Lisbon experience · 4h · All districts

1 / 7
Alfama & Sé Cathedral

Begin in Lisbon's oldest district — medieval streets, tiled facades and the 12th century cathedral that survived the great earthquake of 1755.

2 / 7
Castelo de São Jorge

The ancient Moorish castle crowning Lisbon's highest hill — with 360° views over the entire city, the Tagus and the bridge that echoes San Francisco.

3 / 7
Baixa & Chiado

Through the elegant grid of Baixa — rebuilt after the 1755 earthquake — up into bohemian Chiado, where Lisbon's literary and artistic soul lives.

4 / 7
Belém & Torre

Out to Belém for the iconic Tower and Jerónimos Monastery — the two great monuments of Portugal's Age of Discovery, UNESCO World Heritage.

5 / 7
Praça do Comércio

Back along the riverside to Lisbon's grand ceremonial square — the triumphal arch, the wide promenade and the Tagus glittering in the afternoon light.

6 / 7
Panoramic Viewpoints

The full city tour includes the best miradouros — elevated terraces where all of Lisbon unfolds below you, rooftop by rooftop, river to hills.
