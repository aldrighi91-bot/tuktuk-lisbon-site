3. Booking and Reservations

All bookings are made via WhatsApp or through the booking form on our website. A booking is only confirmed after:

Deposit Policy: A 20% deposit is required to secure your booking. Deposits are processed via SumUp payment link sent via WhatsApp. The remaining balance is due on the day of the tour.

All prices are in Euros (€) and include VAT where applicable:

Prices are per tuk tuk (up to 6 passengers) and are subject to change without prior notice.
